# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 3, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

BridgeAPI provides access to third-party AI model APIs. We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

- **Account information**: Email address for account creation and communication
- **Payment information**: Processed by our payment provider (Creem). We do not store credit card details on our servers
- **Usage data**: API request logs including model used, token consumption, and timestamps for billing purposes
- **API keys**: Encrypted and stored securely for authentication

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

- Processing API requests and billing
- Providing customer support
- Improving service reliability and performance
- Sending important service notifications

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share data with:

- **Payment processors** (Creem/Stripe) — to process transactions
- **Upstream API providers** — only the necessary data to route your requests to the correct AI model

## 4. Data Retention

- **Usage logs**: Retained for 90 days for billing and support purposes
- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active, plus 30 days after closure
- **Payment records**: Retained as required by tax regulations (typically 7 years)

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Request a copy of your data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Opt out of non-essential communications
- Report concerns to our support team

## 6. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us at: support@bridgeapi.io
